About

Who We Are

We are globetrotters who have often been in the situation to find our way in a new, unknown environment. We want to simplify this process in order to facilitate a fast integration. We think this is a prerequisite to feel comfortable in an unknown city as soon as possible.

What We Do

We provide you with all the information you need to get started. Adapted to your individual needs. From visas and insurance to the proper fitness club you will find a number of categories.

How do we it

You tell us who you are, and we'll tell you what you need. It's that simple. Thanks to our database, we can assign your personal data to the right information package. Don't worry, we won't share your files with anyone.
